## **P2. 30-second summary**

This poster describes the development of a **mobile contraception decision aid** intended to support **transgender and gender-nonconforming (TGNC) individuals assigned female at birth (AFAB)** in selecting contraceptive options. The team redesigned an existing desktop tool into a mobile experience using **user-centered design** methods and implemented a web-based system (Node.js backend; Next.js/TypeScript/Tailwind CSS frontend) with plans for deployment on AWS. The tool centers on three user-facing functions: exploring contraceptive methods via descriptions/FAQs, generating personalized recommendations based on medical conditions and preferences, and comparing methods by decision factors. The authors report ongoing iteration focused on visual design, navigation improvements, and expanding health information, supported by an AHRQ grant and ASU IRB approval.
