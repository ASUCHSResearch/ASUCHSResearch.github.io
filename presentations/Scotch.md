## **P10. Glossary**

* **Genomic epidemiology:** Using pathogen genetic sequences to infer spread patterns and transmission pathways.  
* **Flyway:** Migratory route for birds (Pacific, Central, Mississippi referenced).  
* **Subclade K:** A labeled influenza subclade highlighted as rapidly expanding on campus (details not defined on poster).  
* **Phylodynamics:** Use of phylogenetic methods to infer transmission and epidemic dynamics (term used on poster).  
* **Bayes factor:** A Bayesian measure of support for one hypothesis/model vs another; used here for ‚Äúpairwise transmission.‚Äù  
* **HA:** Hemagglutinin (influenza surface protein; H5 HA referenced).  
* **RBD:** Receptor-binding domain (part of HA; substitutions in RBD mentioned).  
* **Antigenic drift:** Accumulation of antigenically relevant mutations (10 HA substitutions noted).  
* **VE:** Vaccine effectiveness (epitope model predicts low VE value 0.13; interpretation scale not defined on poster).  
* **Cryptic transmission:** Circulation that occurs without detection (6‚Äì16 months inferred).
