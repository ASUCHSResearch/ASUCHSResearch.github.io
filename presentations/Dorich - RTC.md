## **P2. 30-second summary**

This poster describes the Research Technology Core (RTC) at ASU’s College of Health Solutions and how it supports health research through custom mobile apps, participant-management web apps, and integrated wearable/IoT platforms. The RTC emphasizes minimally invasive, at-home participation, real-time feedback, and scalable systems that remain compliant with HIPAA and FDA standards. The poster highlights example solutions (walkability app, sleep/CPAP reports, screen-time behavior tools, Fitbit \+ Shelly plug integration) and reports cumulative achievements: 10,000+ participants served, 66 projects deployed, and 5 funded projects in the pipeline.
